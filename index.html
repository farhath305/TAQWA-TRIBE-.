Science Has Limits

Science has given us medicine, rockets, and AI. But it has limits. We can't hear all sounds, see all light, or detect many frequencies passing through us. We've explored less than 5% of the ocean and don’t understand 95% of the universe’s dark matter. Even the brain, the most powerful known computer, remains a mystery.
